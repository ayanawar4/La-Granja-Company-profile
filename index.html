Who We Are

About La Granja

La Granja is a development company redefining the way people live by creating agricultural compounds that unite modern homes with fertile farmland. We offer families a lifestyle that is healthier, more balanced, and closer to nature — without giving up the comfort and convenience of city life.
  • Each La Granja community is designed around a simple idea: a home that gives back. Residents enjoy the beauty of contemporary villas while benefiting from productive land that can generate income.
  • Our compounds offer a lifestyle as sustainable as it is rewarding — where every home is surrounded by fertile farmland.
  • We are lifestyle creators, offering spaces where people can live better today while building value for the future.
  • La Granja was born to turn a vision into reality — agriculture, sustainability, and modern life together, just an hour from the city.

National Context

Egypt's Agricultural Future

Egypt is shaping a new agricultural era — and La Granja is at the heart of it.

Egypt's Agricultural Ambitions

Egypt is investing heavily in agriculture to secure food supply and boost exports. The FY 2025/2026 plan allocates EGP 17.5 billion to modernize farming, increase productivity and expand greenhouses.

The strategy includes reclaiming land to reach 12 million feddans by 2030, using smart irrigation, high-yield crops, and major reclamation projects like Toshka, the New Delta, and Sinai.

Egypt's Vision 2030 further emphasizes technology-driven, sustainable farming through AI, modern irrigation, and climate-adaptive practices.

La Granja: Investing in Egypt's Agricultural Tomorrow

As Egypt embraces a modern, sustainable agricultural future, La Granja stands at the forefront — combining contemporary living with productive farmland. Every home is part of a living ecosystem where comfort, nature, and effortless income coexist.

Just outside Cairo, La Granja offers rural tranquility with city accessibility, while certified green contracts ensure secure, long-term investment — making it a model for Egypt's next generation of agriculture.

About Sun Gate
La Granja is proud to partner with Sun Gate, one of Egypt's leading names in food production, trading, and export. Sun Gate's Vision
To become the leading and preferred export with quality, service, and values as the highest priorities — satisfying the needs of customers, employees, and stakeholders.
From Farm to Market — Seamlessly
La Granja supplies Sun Gate with fresh, high-quality crops grown on our farms. Sun Gate then processes, packages, and distributes these products across local and global markets. Together, we ensure a seamless supply chain from farm to final product.
